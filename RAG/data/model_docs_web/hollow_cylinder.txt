# hollow_cylinder — SasView 6.1.0 documentation

Model: hollow_cylinder
URL: https://www.sasview.org/docs/user/models/hollow_cylinder.html

## Parameters
- **scale**: Scale factor or Volume fraction
  Units: None
- **background**: Source background
  Units: cm-1
- **radius**: Cylinder core radius
  Units: Å
- **thickness**: Cylinder wall thickness
  Units: Å
- **length**: Cylinder total length
  Units: Å
- **sld**: Cylinder sld
  Units: 10-6Å-2
- **sld_solvent**: Solvent sld
  Units: 10-6Å-2
- **theta**: Cylinder axis to beam angle
  Units: degree
- **phi**: Rotation about beam
  Units: degree

## Equations
\[P(q) = \text{scale} \left<F^2\right>/V_\text{shell} + \text{background}\]
\[\begin{split}P(q)           &= (\text{scale})V_\text{shell}\Delta\rho^2
        \int_0^{1}\Psi^2
        \left[q_z, R_\text{outer}(1-x^2)^{1/2},
                   R_\text{core}(1-x^2)^{1/2}\right]
        \left[\frac{\sin(qHx)}{qHx}\right]^2 dx \\
\Psi[q,y,z]    &= \frac{1}{1-\gamma^2}
        \left[ \Lambda(qy) - \gamma^2\Lambda(qz) \right] \\
\Lambda(a)     &= 2 J_1(a) / a \\
\gamma         &= R_\text{core} / R_\text{outer} \\
V_\text{shell} &= \pi \left(R_\text{outer}^2 - R_\text{core}^2 \right)L \\
J_1(x)         &= (\sin(x)-x\cdot \cos(x)) / x^2\end{split}\]
