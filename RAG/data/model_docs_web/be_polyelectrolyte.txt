## Description
Polyelectrolyte with the RPA expression derived by Borue and Erukhimovich

## Parameters
- **scale**: Scale factor or Volume fraction
  Units: None
- **background**: Source background
  Units: cm-1
- **contrast_factor**: Contrast factor of the polymer
  Units: barns
- **bjerrum_length**: Bjerrum length
  Units: Å
- **virial_param**: Virial parameter
  Units: Å3
- **monomer_length**: Monomer length
  Units: Å
- **salt_concentration**: Concentration of monovalent salt
  Units: mol/L
- **ionization_degree**: Degree of ionization
  Units: None
- **polymer_concentration**: Polymer molar concentration
  Units: mol/L

## Equations
\[\begin{split}I(q) &= K\frac{q^2+k^2}{4\pi L_b\alpha ^2}
    \frac{1}{1+r_{0}^4(q^2+k^2)(q^2-12hC_a/b^2)} + \text{background} \\
k^2 &= 4\pi L_b(2C_s + \alpha C_a) \\
r_{0}^2 &= \frac{b}{\alpha \sqrt{C_a 48\pi L_b}}\end{split}\]
\[\begin{split}K &= a^2 \\
a &= b_p - (v_p/v_s) b_s\end{split}\]
\[q = \sqrt{q_x^2 + q_y^2}\]
