# binary_hard_sphere — SasView 6.1.0 documentation

Model: binary_hard_sphere
URL: https://www.sasview.org/docs/user/models/binary_hard_sphere.html

## Description
binary mixture of hard spheres with hard sphere interactions.

## Parameters
- **scale**: Scale factor or Volume fraction
  Units: None
- **background**: Source background
  Units: cm-1
- **radius_lg**: radius of large particle
  Units: Å
- **radius_sm**: radius of small particle
  Units: Å
- **volfraction_lg**: volume fraction of large particle
  Units: None
- **volfraction_sm**: volume fraction of small particle
  Units: None
- **sld_lg**: scattering length density of large particle
  Units: 10-6Å-2
- **sld_sm**: scattering length density of small particle
  Units: 10-6Å-2
- **sld_solvent**: Solvent scattering length density
  Units: 10-6Å-2

## Equations
\[I(q) = (1-x)f_1^2(q) S_{11}(q) + 2[x(1-x)]^{1/2} f_1(q)f_2(q)S_{12}(q) +
x\,f_2^2(q)S_{22}(q)\]
\begin{align*}
x &= \frac{(\phi_2 / \phi)\alpha^3}{(1-(\phi_2/\phi) + (\phi_2/\phi)
\alpha^3)} \\
\phi &= \phi_1 + \phi_2 = \text{total volume fraction} \\
\alpha &= R_1/R_2 = \text{size ratio}
\end{align*}
\[q = \sqrt{q_x^2 + q_y^2}\]
