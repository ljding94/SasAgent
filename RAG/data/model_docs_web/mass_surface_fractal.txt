# mass_surface_fractal — SasView 6.1.0 documentation

Model: mass_surface_fractal
URL: https://www.sasview.org/docs/user/models/mass_surface_fractal.html

## Parameters
- **scale**: Scale factor or Volume fraction
  Units: None
- **background**: Source background
  Units: cm-1
- **fractal_dim_mass**: Mass fractal dimension
  Units: None
- **fractal_dim_surf**: Surface fractal dimension
  Units: None
- **rg_cluster**: Cluster radius of gyration
  Units: Å
- **rg_primary**: Primary particle radius of gyration
  Units: Å

## Equations
\[\begin{split}I(q) &= \text{scale} \times P(q) + \text{background} \\
P(q) &= \left\{ \left[ 1+(q^2a)\right]^{D_m/2} \times
               \left[ 1+(q^2b)\right]^{(6-D_s-D_m)/2}
       \right\}^{-1} \\
a &= R_{g}^2/(3D_m/2) \\
b &= r_{g}^2/[-3(D_s+D_m-6)/2] \\
\text{scale} &= \text{scale factor}
    \times NV^2 (\rho_\text{particle} - \rho_\text{solvent})^2\end{split}\]
