# poly_gauss_coil — SasView 6.1.0 documentation

Model: poly_gauss_coil
URL: https://www.sasview.org/docs/user/models/poly_gauss_coil.html

## Parameters
- **scale**: Scale factor or Volume fraction
  Units: None
- **background**: Source background
  Units: cm-1
- **i_zero**: Intensity at q=0
  Units: cm-1
- **rg**: Radius of gyration
  Units: Å
- **polydispersity**: Polymer Mw/Mn
  Units: None

## Equations
\[I(q) = \text{scale} \cdot I_0 \cdot P(q) + \text{background}\]
\[\begin{split}I_0 &= \phi_\text{poly} \cdot V \cdot (\rho_\text{poly}-\rho_\text{solv})^2 \\
P(q) &= 2 [(1 + UZ)^{-1/U} + Z - 1] / [(1 + U) Z^2] \\
Z &= [(q R_g)^2] / (1 + 2U) \\
U &= (Mw / Mn) - 1 = \text{polydispersity ratio} - 1 \\
V &= M / (N_A \delta)\end{split}\]
\[q = \sqrt{q_x^2 + q_y^2}\]
