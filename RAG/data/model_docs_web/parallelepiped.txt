# parallelepiped — SasView 6.1.0 documentation

Model: parallelepiped
URL: https://www.sasview.org/docs/user/models/parallelepiped.html

## Description
Rectangular parallelepiped with uniform scattering length density.

## Parameters
- **scale**: Scale factor or Volume fraction
  Units: None
- **background**: Source background
  Units: cm-1
- **sld**: Parallelepiped scattering length density
  Units: 10-6Å-2
- **sld_solvent**: Solvent scattering length density
  Units: 10-6Å-2
- **length_a**: Shorter side of the parallelepiped
  Units: Å
- **length_b**: Second side of the parallelepiped
  Units: Å
- **length_c**: Larger side of the parallelepiped
  Units: Å
- **theta**: c axis to beam angle
  Units: degree
- **phi**: rotation about beam
  Units: degree
- **psi**: rotation about c axis
  Units: degree

## Equations
\[I(q) = \frac{\text{scale}}{V} (\Delta\rho \cdot V)^2
       \left< P(q, \alpha, \beta) \right> + \text{background}\]
\[P(q, \alpha) = \int_0^1 \phi_Q\left(\mu \sqrt{1-\sigma^2},a\right)
    \left[S(\mu c \sigma/2)\right]^2 d\sigma\]
\[\begin{split}\phi_Q(\mu,a) &= \int_0^1
    \left\{S\left[\frac{\mu}{2}\cos\left(\frac{\pi}{2}u\right)\right]
           S\left[\frac{\mu a}{2}\sin\left(\frac{\pi}{2}u\right)\right]
           \right\}^2 du \\
S(x) &= \frac{\sin x}{x} \\
\mu &= qB\end{split}\]
\[I(q_x, q_y) = \frac{\text{scale}}{V} (\Delta\rho \cdot V)^2 P(q_x, q_y)
        + \text{background}\]
\[P(q_x, q_y) = \left[\frac{\sin(\tfrac{1}{2}qA\cos\alpha)}{(\tfrac{1}
               {2}qA\cos\alpha)}\right]^2
              \left[\frac{\sin(\tfrac{1}{2}qB\cos\beta)}{(\tfrac{1}
               {2}qB\cos\beta)}\right]^2
              \left[\frac{\sin(\tfrac{1}{2}qC\cos\gamma)}{(\tfrac{1}
               {2}qC\cos\gamma)}\right]^2\]
\[\begin{split}\cos\alpha &= \hat A \cdot \hat q, \\
\cos\beta  &= \hat B \cdot \hat q, \\
\cos\gamma &= \hat C \cdot \hat q\end{split}\]
